MOST LOVED TEAMMATES

Kakashi Hatake

is a shinobi of Konohagakure's Hatake clan. Famed as Kakashi of the Sharingan, he is one of Konoha's most talented ninja. To his students on Team 7, Kakashi emphasises the importance of teamwork. After the Fourth Shinobi World War, Kakashi becomes Konoha's Sixth Hokage (六代目火影, Rokudaime Hokage, literally meaning: Sixth Fire Shadow).

Kaka-sensei

Naruto Uzumaki

became the jinchūriki of the Nine-Tails on the day of his birth. Through many hardships and ordeals, he became a capable ninja regarded as the Hero of the Hidden Leaf. He soon proved to be one of the main factors in winning the Fourth Shinobi World War, leading him to achieve his dream and become the village's Seventh Hokage (七代目火影, Nanadaime Hokage, lit. Seventh Fire Shadow).

Naruto-baka

Sakura Haruno

is a kunoichi of Konohagakure. When assigned to Team 7, Sakura quickly finds herself ill-prepared for the duties of a shinobi. However, after training under the Sannin Tsunade, she overcomes this, and becomes recognised as one of the greatest medical-nin in the world.

Sakura-chan

Sasuke Uchiha

After learning the truth of his brother's sacrifice, later proving instrumental in ending the Fourth Shinobi World War, Sasuke decides to return to Konoha and dedicate his life to help protect the village and its inhabitants, becoming referred to as the "Supporting Kage" (支う影, Sasaukage, literally meaning: Supporting Shadow).
